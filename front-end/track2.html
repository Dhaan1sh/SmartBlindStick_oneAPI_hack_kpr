GPS Tracking

Our advanced GPS tracking system provides real-time location information, ensuring the safety of the user. Caregivers can monitor the location through our web interface or mobile app.

Obstacle Detection

Using state-of-the-art ultrasonic sensors, the Smart Blind Stick detects obstacles in the user's path and provides tactile feedback, enhancing mobility and safety.

Emergency Alert System

With a simple button press, users can send immediate alerts to pre-designated emergency contacts, ensuring help is always at hand when needed.

Voice Navigation

Integrated voice navigation provides turn-by-turn directions and important environmental information, helping users navigate with confidence.

Weather Resistant

Help & FAQ

How do I activate the geofence?

Click the "Toggle Geofence" button on the home page to activate or deactivate the geofence.

What should I do if the battery is low?

Charge the smart stick as soon as possible. The system will send an automatic alert when the battery level drops below 20%.

How can I update the emergency contact information?

Please contact our support team to update the emergency contact details for your device.

What do I do in case of an emergency?

Press the "Send Help Alert" button on the home page or use the physical button on the smart stick to send an immediate alert to the registered emergency contact.
